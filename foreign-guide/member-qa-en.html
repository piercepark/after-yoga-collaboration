Frequently Asked Questions

Q&A
Everything You Need to Know
14 questions · English only
Q1
Do I need experience to join?
Answer
Not at all. Many members start as complete beginners. Our instructors explain slowly and clearly — you can always follow at your own pace.
Q2
What programs do you offer?
Answer
Both yoga and pilates. Yoga includes traditional, modern, bungee, and flying styles. Pilates is small-group, equipment-based training.
Q3
What is the class size?
Answer
  • Pilates: max 6 people per class
  • Yoga: up to 20 people per class
Q4
How long is each class?
Answer
  • Pilates: 50 minutes
  • Yoga: 60 minutes
Q5
What types of pilates classes are there?
Answer
Four focus areas — the class depends on the day and program:
  • Posture correction
  • Core strengthening
  • Glute-focused training
  • Weight-management sessions
Q6
Why do yoga classes change every other week?
Answer
We rotate programs biweekly to keep training fresh and balanced. Odd weeks (Wk 3, 5 in May) differ from even weeks (Wk 2, 4).
Q7
When are classes available?
Answer
Most classes run in the morning and evening. Choose morning if you're free early, or evening to unwind after work.
Q8
What membership types do you have?
Answer
  • Yoga-only plan
  • Pilates-only plan
  • Combined plan (yoga + pilates)
Q9
What membership lengths are available?
Answer
All plans are available in 3, 6, 12, and 24 months. We provide a slightly longer usage period than the basic duration.
Q10
How do sessions work? Is it unlimited?
Answer
Each membership includes a set number of sessions — more than a standard twice-a-week plan. Use them freely anytime. Your membership ends when either the time period or all sessions run out, whichever comes first.
Q11
How does the combined plan ratio work?
Answer
Sessions split in a 2:1 ratio (yoga 2 : pilates 1). Pilates classes are capped at 6 people, so this ratio keeps reservations fair for everyone.
Q12
How do I book or cancel classes?
Answer
Through our app "Studio Mate." After registration:
  • Scan the QR code & install the app
  • Sign up with your membership info
  • Check your schedule & book classes
  • App sends reminders & cancellation rules
Q13
Can I use the showers and changing rooms?
Answer
Yes — showers and changing rooms are available for all members.
Q14
If I have questions later, who should I ask?
Answer
Ask the manager or staff at the front desk anytime, or message us through the Studio Mate app.
